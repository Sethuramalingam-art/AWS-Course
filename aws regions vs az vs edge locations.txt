AWS regions
Larger geographic areas with multiple availability zones. When building a solution for a specific geographic area, you select the region to build the solution in.
AWS availability zones
A group of data centers that are isolated from failures in other availability zones. They are used for hosting servers, websites, applications, software, and more.
AWS edge locations
Mini data centers that are spread out geographically to ensure fast delivery of content to users around the world. They are primarily used for caching data to provide a better user experience with low latency.
